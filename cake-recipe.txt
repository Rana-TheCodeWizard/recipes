Ingredients:
4 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
2-1/4 teaspoons baking powder
1-1/4 cups 2% milk
10 tablespoons butter, cubed

Directions
In a large bowl, beat eggs on high speed for 5 minutes or until thick and lemon-colored. Gradually add sugar, beating until mixture is light and fluffy. Beat in vanilla. Combine flour and baking powder; gradually add to batter; beat at low speed until smooth.
In a small saucepan, heat milk and butter just until butter is melted. Gradually add to batter; beat just until combined.
Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking pan. Bake at 350Â° for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack. Yield: 12-16 servings.


Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 3/4 cup cocoa
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 1 cup boiling water

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda
    and salt.  Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in
    boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
